SUBJECT: 2d Marine Logistics Group Annual CUI Refresher Training Memorandum

1. PURPOSE. This memorandum directs all 2d MLG personnel — military, civilian, and embedded contractor — to complete Annual CUI Refresher Training NLT 30 June 2026 in accordance with DoDM 5200.01 Vol 2 and 32 CFR Part 2002.

2. APPLICABILITY. All personnel assigned, attached, or providing direct support to 2d MLG. Contractor personnel will complete training under the data-handling clauses of their contract and will provide the contracting officer's representative with a completion certificate.

3. TRAINING CONTENT. The training module covers: (a) the CUI program lifecycle, (b) lawful-government-purpose disclosure standards, (c) the difference between CUI Basic and CUI Specified, (d) marking mechanics including banner lines and portion marks, and (e) destruction standards under NIST SP 800-88. None of this content is itself controlled.

4. ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES. The training is hosted on the unclassified MarineNet learning management system. There are no PII fields, no force-protection indicators, no proprietary contractor data, and no operational details in this memorandum. The memorandum is intended for unrestricted internal distribution and posting on the 2d MLG SharePoint announcements page.

5. POINT OF CONTACT. Direct questions to the 2d MLG Information Protection Officer through the chain of command. The IPO mailbox is published on the public 2d MLG portal.

6. DISTRIBUTION. Unrestricted internal distribution. May be reposted on the public 2d MLG website if no banner-line marking is applied at the page level.
