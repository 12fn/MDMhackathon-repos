SUBJECT: Exercise STEEL HORIZON 26-3 Operations Brief — Draft v0.4

1. PURPOSE. This brief outlines the concept of operations for Exercise STEEL HORIZON 26-3, a III MEF combined-arms rehearsal scheduled vicinity Camp Pendleton 12-19 May 2026. Audience: III MEF G-3, MARFORPAC plans, and coalition liaison officers from Australia (AUS) and the United Kingdom (GBR).

2. PARTICIPATING UNITS. 1st Marine Division (-) (Reinforced) with attachments from 1st MAW and 1st MLG. Coalition contingent: 3 RAR (AUS, 88 personnel) and 42 Commando (GBR, 64 personnel). Notional opposing force is portrayed by 1st Bn 4th Marines acting as a near-peer mechanized infantry battalion.

3. FORCE PROTECTION POSTURE. FPCON BRAVO will be in effect throughout the exercise. The vulnerability assessment identifies the western motor pool gate and the unmarked range entry on Old Stage Road as the two highest-risk ingress points. Manning roster for the entry control points is at Annex C; rotation schedule is attached at Annex D and is not to be released outside the III MEF FP cell.

4. INTELLIGENCE SUPPORT. 1st Intelligence Battalion will provide a tailored intel package built from open-source reporting and previously-released exercise products. No classified intelligence is required for this rehearsal. The G-2 estimate for the OPFOR cell uses publicly-available platform data on the BMP-3 and T-72B3 for vehicle role-play.

5. LOGISTICS. Class I, III, and V supply will be drawn from MCLB Barstow against existing exercise allocations. Combat Service Support Detachment 11 will operate the forward distribution point. There are no proprietary contractor rate cards or vendor cost build-ups in this brief; all support is organic.

6. RELEASABILITY INTENT. The author requests this brief be marked at the lowest level consistent with operational security, with appropriate caveat to permit coalition LNO read-in. Final marking is at the discretion of the III MEF G-2 OCA-equivalent reviewer.
